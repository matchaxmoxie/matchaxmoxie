## Canvas paste (source)

During office hours, FASE Peer Mentors are expected to make best use of their time. Peer mentors are expected to be on time and check in with administrative coordinator using the iPads located in the lobby of 300 N. Eagleson. Unless otherwise agreed upon, office hours should be held in the assigned office location at 300 N. Eagleson.

**Fall 2026 schedules:** see [Fall Office Hour Schedules](../../site/fall-office-hours.html) in Important Info for your assigned day and time (mentor grid and SPC/MAP grid).

Examples of expected actions in offices hours include, but are not limited to:

- Meeting with proteges/students
- Updating Kuali Time
- Updating/submitting Session Summaries
- Sending/checking FASE-related emails
- Completing various tasks given by an SPC, MAP Coordinator, or a FASE Office Staff member
- Updating protege Canvas page
- Attending monthly SPC meetings
- Researching events and resources to support proteges
- Planning Lunch & Learns and other FASE-related events

### Missing Office Hours

If you need to be absent from your scheduled office hours due to a FASE-approved conflict (more about that later), you MUST reach out to the SPCs, executive director, and the administrative coordinator at least 24 hours in advance.

Reach out in the FASE GroupMe to find another mentor who will cover your hours or switch office hour times with you that week. Notify the SPCs, executive director, and the administrative coordinator if you are able to find coverage, CCing the mentor who has agreed to cover. If no one is able to cover your shift, let the SPCs, executive director, and the administrative coordinator know that you were unable to find coverage.
