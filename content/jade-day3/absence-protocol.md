## FASE events take priority

FASE events take priority over Greek life, club, sporting events, and additional jobs.

## Approved absence types

- Class, lab, or discussion conflicts
- Professor-mandated exam reviews
- Exams
- Required class office hours
- Grad school or professional certification prep
- Career interviews
- Illness
- Doctor appointments
- Bad weather or safety concerns
- Other with executive director approval

## Advance notice (planned absence)

Email the SPCs, executive director, and administrative coordinator **at least 24 hours before** the event. Include:

- Event title
- Date and time
- Reason for absence

## Unforeseen absence (within 24 hours)

If something unforeseen comes up within 24 hours of the event, still notify the SPCs, executive director, and administrative coordinator as soon as possible.

## Unexcused or unapproved absences

Two or more unexcused or unapproved absences per semester will result in a meeting with the executive director.

## Video / script outline

Record a mentor-facing walkthrough covering:

5. Consequence of two+ unexcused or unapproved absences per semester
