## Canvas paste (source)

### What are SPC meetings?

SPC meetings bring peer mentors and SPCs together for professional development — not staff lectures. Mentors share how they support protégés, exchange event ideas, and stay aligned as a cohort.

### Why attendance matters

The [FASE Office Hours](office-hours.md) page lists **monthly SPC meetings** alongside meeting protégés, updating Kuali Time, and planning events. When you come prepared, you learn from other mentors and help keep the cohort coordinated instead of everyone working in isolation.

SPC meetings carry the same priority as in-services and MSLD events — before Greek life, clubs, sports, and extra jobs.

### Schedule and location

Meetings happen **monthly**. Peer mentors and SPCs attend.

**Fall 2026:** exact dates, room, and hybrid link are being confirmed with SPC leads. Check this Canvas page, the Fall FASE calendar when posted, and GroupMe messages from your SPC before your first meeting.

### What to prepare

Format and agenda items are still being set for this year — whether you bring protégé updates, event plans, or questions for the group. **Ask your SPC** what to prepare before each meeting.

### If you cannot attend

Follow the [Absence Protocol](absence-protocol.md) — same email rules, same **24-hour advance notice** for planned conflicts.
