## 1 — Hook

**VO:** SPC meetings bring peer mentors and SPCs together for professional development — not staff lectures. Mentors share how they support protégés, exchange event ideas, and stay aligned as a cohort.

**B-ROLL:** Wide of mentors and SPCs in a meeting room; candid shots of discussion, not a presenter to camera.

---

## 2 — Why attendance matters

**VO:** The FASE Office Hours page lists monthly SPC meetings alongside meeting protégés, updating Kuali Time, and planning events. When you come prepared, you learn from other mentors and help keep the cohort coordinated instead of everyone working in isolation.

**B-ROLL:** On-screen excerpt from Office Hours page: "Attending monthly SPC meetings." Optional Kuali Time or event-planning B-roll.

---

## 3 — What we know now

**VO:** Meetings happen monthly, and peer mentors and SPCs attend. They carry the same priority as in-services and MSLD events — before Greek life, clubs, sports, and extra jobs. Exact schedule, location, hybrid link, and what to prepare are still being confirmed with SPC leads. Please check this Canvas page and your GroupMe before your first meeting.

**B-ROLL:** Calendar with monthly markers; on-screen note — *Confirm Fall 2026 schedule and location with SPC leads before publishing final cut.* GroupMe notification mockup.

---

## 4 — If you can't make it

**VO:** If you cannot make a meeting, please follow the Absence Protocol in this module — same email rules, same twenty-four-hour advance notice for planned conflicts.

**B-ROLL:** Link thumb or highlight to Absence Protocol page; email mockup with To line and three bullet body.

---

## 5 — What to expect

**VO:** Format and agenda items are still being set for this year — whether you bring protégé updates, event plans, or questions for the group. Your SPC is the best person to ask about prep. Even where details are still being finalized, you are learning from coordinators who have been in your role.

**B-ROLL:** Mentors sharing notes; whiteboard or agenda slide placeholder; SPC lead in B-roll (no dialogue on mic).

---

## 6 — When and where

**VO:** For the next meeting, check this page, the Fall FASE calendar when it is posted, and messages from your SPC. If anything in this video does not match what is live in Canvas, trust the page and your SPC over this recording.

**B-ROLL:** Canvas SPC Meetings page scroll; Fall FASE calendar placeholder; GroupMe message mockup.

---

## 7 — Close

**VO:** Ask your SPC what to prepare, and come ready to share how you are supporting protégés and what events you are planning. Your cohort will be glad you showed up.

**B-ROLL:** Warm group shot of mentors leaving a meeting; fade on Canvas page URL or module nav.

- **Tone:** Professional peer coordinator — engaging, kind, and collegial; clear without being lecture or casual vlog.
- **Mic:** Record VO in one session for consistent tone; re-record Sections 3 and 5 after SPC lead confirm.
- **Pace:** Steady and conversational — do not rush the absence-protocol block.
- **Music:** Optional light bed under B-roll only; none under VO.
- **Edit:** Swap calendar B-roll once Fall 2026 dates are confirmed; add on-screen supers for room and hybrid link.
