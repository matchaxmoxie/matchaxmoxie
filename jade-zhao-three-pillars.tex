My work is grounded in practical impact: how tools behave in real contexts, how teams adopt them, and how outcomes show up in measurable progress. The following pillars organize how I prioritize projects and collaboration.

\section*{Three Pillars of Success (Tech for Good)}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Community Building}

    Build tools and programs that create belonging and reduce friction for users and teams. For example, I supported first-generation and low-income students by consolidating fragmented campus resources into accessible digital tools, and I helped deliver learning experiences through structured mentorship.

    \item \textbf{Technical Excellence}

    Deliver solutions that work reliably in real contexts. I have developed HIPAA-compliant database systems, built real-time patient monitoring workflows, and implemented accessibility-focused improvements across websites with measurable impact.

    \item \textbf{Equity and Inclusion}

    Prioritize accessibility and inclusive outcomes from day one. I conduct accessibility audits (WCAG 2.1 AA/AAA) and translate findings into implementation roadmaps so teams can maintain compliance over time.
\end{enumerate}
