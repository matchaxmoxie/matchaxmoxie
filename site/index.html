Jade Zhao at Indiana University

Hi. I am Jade Zhao, a first-gen student at Indiana University’s Luddy School (Informatics, Class of 2027 · May graduation). This sunset site is the classroom twin of my sunrise portfolio on jadexzhao.github.io.

I grew up in a busy family restaurant. High pressure, chaotic logistics, human behavior all at once. That same urgency shows up when I teach and when I ship code. On the sunrise side I also give back through ServeIT (IU Luddy’s nonprofit tech clinic; ServeAI is the PIT-UN public-interest AI track): accessible builds for Bloomington partners. 福州 roots. why teaching stuck

I always had a passion for teaching because of my high school Asian American robotics teacher. She taught me that engineering is for everyone. I still believe that.

Girls Who Code was where that belief became a classroom. Web Designer & Instructor · Indianapolis · Jan 2018 to Aug 2023. Instructed 50+ students. Facilitated 12 student-led projects. Mentored 20+ young women toward STEM.
