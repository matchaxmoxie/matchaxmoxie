what this year was about

Direct admit to Luddy. Computers execute instructions, not intentions: that landed hard in Introduction to Computers and Programming (CSCI-C 200) and stayed with me. Introduction to Informatics (INFO-I 101) and Interaction Design Thinking (INFO-I 245) connected systems to people. Spring brought usability, social informatics, and ServeIT (INFO-I 389): first clinic credit shipping for real partners.

Summer finite math (MATH-M 118) closed the year. GWC (Girls Who Code) teaching energy from high school was still in the room; Miss Zhao (classroom teacher nickname) craft would keep growing on this twin.
