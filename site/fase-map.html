About me

Hi, I'm Jade Zhao, a senior at IU's (Indiana University's) Luddy School of Informatics and a MAP (Mentoring Apprenticeship Program) Coordinator with FASE. I grew up in Indianapolis, Indiana helping run my family's Chinese restaurant, where I learned that showing up for the people around you is everything. I'm first-gen too, so I know what the beginning of college feels like. I joined FASE because I want to make that experience a little less overwhelming for every student in the program.

Outside of FASE, I do undergraduate research, build websites for nonprofits, and am very seriously pursuing my long-term dream of owning a duck farm.
