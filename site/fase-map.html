about me

hey. i'm jade zhao, senior at iu's luddy school of informatics and MAP (mentoring apprenticeship program) coordinator with FASE. this is the @matchaxmoxie side: brand, ops, and making sure mentors actually have what they need on day one.

福州 fuzhou, fujian roots. grew up helping run my family's chinese restaurant in indiana. the only metric that mattered was whether the food hit the table hot. that's where i learned showing up for people is the whole job. first-gen too, so i know what the first week of college feels like when nobody in your family has done it before. i joined FASE because i want that path a little less overwhelming for every student in the program.

outside FASE: i lead serveit's website team (iu's nonprofit tech clinic. wcag builds that actually ship), coordinate canvas ops for MAP, and co-run two github accounts with myself. @jadexzhao writes the code, @matchaxmoxie runs the brand. and yes, i am very seriously pursuing the long-term dream of owning a duck farm. 🦆 鸭年 2026. if you know, you know.
