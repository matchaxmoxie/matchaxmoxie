demo engineering taxonomy

sales sandboxes · product rigor

mini runbook · principles and checklists, not a product claim sheet

restaurant kid wiring: if the pitch stalls mid-flow, the demo env failed the shift.

a demo is a product feature with a deadline. i treat sales sandboxes like anything i would hand a serveit partner or a fase mentor: isolate it, mirror the real shape without the secrets, make it resetable, and leave a path the next person can re-run cold.

界 scope

what this is (and is not)

this page is a taxonomy of how i architect demos. it draws on known work: restaurant/i18n production shapes, serveit handoffs that must survive without me on call, fase canvas publish loops, and gtm sandbox thinking (stealth yc intern lane: pitch → working pilot). it does not invent proprietary internals, name a stealth employer, or claim lab/IRB systems.

proof of interactive sandbox discipline lives on the briefcase: virtual duck farm. product governance lives in how i work.

法 taxonomy

five principles · checklist form

same sandwich rule as product: systems do what you wrote. demos fail when assumptions stay silent.

  1. tenant / demo isolation one sandbox per story. synthetic data preferred; sanitized copies only when the story requires real shape. never leak production PII into a pitch environment. restaurant memory: you do not seat a reviewer at the staff desk with the night deposit still open.
  2. mirror production shape, not production secrets schemas, flows, and empty-state copy should feel like the real product. credentials, private keys, partner dumps, and staff-only ops stay out. if the demo needs a "customer," invent one with a loud fake name so nobody mistakes it for live data.
  3. reset / reseed for repeatable demos every AE run starts from the same known seed. document the reseed button, script, or checklist step. if the second showing depends on leftover state from the first, the demo is not finished.
  4. observability for "did the pitch stall here?" instrument the happy path enough to answer where a prospect hesitated: which screen, which empty state, which form. this is not product analytics for vanity. it is a post-call note so the deck and the env stay honest with each other.
  5. handoff doc so an AE can re-run cold owner, prerequisites, seed state, click path, known brittle edges, and what "done" looks like. same standard as the fase handoff manifesto and the open handoff checklist template.
检 preflight

pre-call checklist

  • isolation: sandbox URL/tenant is not prod; sample rows are labeled synthetic or clearly sanitized.
  • shape: flows match the pitch story; secrets and staff tools are off the path.
  • reseed: reset ran within the last hour (or auto-reset is verified).
  • stall signals: you know which two steps to watch if energy drops.
  • ae packet: one-pager with click path + rollback exists outside your head.
