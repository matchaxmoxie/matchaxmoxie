the sandwich stack

every ops surface needs visible layers. no mystery filling.

  1. bread (baseline): who owns this, what triggers it, what done looks like.
  2. peanut butter (coverage): prerequisites chained in order. no skipped edges.
  3. jelly (signal): status checks, blast radius, who gets pinged when it breaks.
  4. top bread (handoff): a path the next person can run cold at 9pm.
