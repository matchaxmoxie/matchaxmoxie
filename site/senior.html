Theoria et Praxis (Theory and Practice)

from freshman to senior year, one thing stayed constant:

precision generates trust.

not just in code. in research, in mentorship, in leadership, in every system that involves another person relying on your work.

the sandwich was never about sandwiches.

it was about the standard you hold yourself to - say what you mean, completely, in the right order, with no steps missing.

and then hand it to someone else and trust them to run it. 🌷
